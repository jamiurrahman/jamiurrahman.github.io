3D Scanning and Motion Capture

I generated 3D meshes from implicit surfaces using Marching Cubes (MC), Hoppe and Radial Basis Functions (RBF). Then applied Procrustes Algorithm to align two meshes of a same object and solved nonlinear optimization problem with Ceres-solver.
